About

The ICTAK Alumni Job Portal is a web-based application that is helpful for users, alumni, employers as well as the ICTAK authorities. The main objective of this system is to design or develop a software portal for ICTAK To study the existing system Searching for more job seekers ( clients) is made easier, reduces manual work, To develop a suitable system for Job entries and searches, to reduce errors at the time of Job entries and search.
